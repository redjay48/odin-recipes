Uncle Roger Fried Rice

Description

What started out probably as a meme, is now an Internet Phenomenon. But I guess all memes are Internet Phenomenon. Anyway, I digress. We are talking about Uncle Roger of course. His quintessential dissing of celebrity chefs and their archaic but no less self-righteous twists to classic recipes that bafles our favourite Asian Uncle and in all fairness, us too. You're not alone, Uncle Roger. As someone who picks on others' recipes for a living, I wonder how good he is at this art himself.

Today We will be tackling one of those recipes The Internet has relentlessly obsessed over. I know I certainly have. And to make it more exciting, the recipe is none other than Uncle Roger's.

What makes this recipe so loved is not only the simplicity but the availability of the ingredients and how it requires just a single pan and absolutely no time to cook. Not to mention the sauce that brings it all together is what makes you come back for seconds. Now let's begin!
